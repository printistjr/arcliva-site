Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 24, 2026

Contact: [support@arcliva.com]

Arcliva (“we,” “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it when you use our website and dashboard.

dataset What we collect

  • • Account info: name, email, login details
  • • Purchase info: basic purchase status and transaction details (payments are processed by third parties; we do not store full card numbers)
  • • Messages you send: including messages to Ask Arcliva and Arcliva Clarity™
  • • Basic usage data: device/browser info, IP address, and pages/actions (via cookies and similar tools)

insights How we use it

We use this information to:

  • • provide access to your account and purchased dashboard
  • • process purchases and send access emails
  • • respond to support requests
  • • improve the website, dashboard, and AI responses
  • • prevent fraud and misuse

psychology AI tools

If you use Ask Arcliva or Arcliva Clarity™, your messages may be stored to provide responses, improve quality and safety, and prevent abuse. Do not submit sensitive personal information you do not want stored.

shopping_cart Affiliate links

The Shopping List may include affiliate links (example: Amazon). If you click a link, the retailer may use cookies or tracking on their site. Arcliva does not control third-party websites.

cookie Cookies

We use cookies and similar tools for basic site function and analytics. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Some features may not work properly if cookies are disabled.

share Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers who help us operate the website and dashboard (hosting, email, analytics, payments), and when required by law.

verified_user Your privacy rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • • request access to the personal information we have about you
  • • request correction or deletion of your personal information
  • • opt out of marketing emails (use the unsubscribe link in any email)

Do Not Sell/Share:

Arcliva does not sell your personal information.

To make a request, contact [support@arcliva.com]. We may need to verify your identity before completing your request.

history Data retention

We keep data only as long as needed to provide the service, maintain records, and improve safety.

update Updates

We may update this policy. The effective date will be updated when changes are made.
