Common Questions

Is there a free trial? expand_more

We don't offer a free trial, but every plan is backed by the 7-Day Clarity Guarantee. If you don't get clarity on what to take, when to take it, and why it matters within 7 days, request a full refund — no questions asked.

Can I upgrade from monthly to lifetime later? expand_more

Yes. You can upgrade at any time. Just reach out and we'll apply a prorated credit toward lifetime access based on how long you've been a monthly or annual member.

What happens when I cancel monthly or annual? expand_more

Your access continues through the end of your current billing period. After that, you lose access to the app. Your data is retained for 90 days if you ever want to reactivate.

Do I need to buy supplements separately? expand_more

Yes. Arcliva is a digital program — supplement protocols, routines, guidance, and the AI assistant. The supplements themselves are purchased separately. We provide smart shopping lists with direct links to help you find and compare them.

What does "lifetime" mean exactly? expand_more

Lifetime means access for as long as Arcliva exists as a product — including all content updates, new system additions, and AI improvements. One payment, no further charges, no renewals.
